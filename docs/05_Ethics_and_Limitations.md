# Limitations and Ethical Considerations

* **Data Privacy**: Explicit informed consent must be obtained for analyzing personal and public behavioral data. The system utilizes built-in ethical logic, encryption, and anonymization.
* **Bias Risk**: Sentiment models may misclassify emotion depending on language, slang, and cultural context. Multilingual embeddings are proposed to mitigate this.
* **False Positives/Negatives**: Incorrect predictions could increase stress or cause dependency. The system maintains fallback functions to return "Low" risk in case of processing errors to avoid false panics.
* **Ethical Safeguards**: The system includes emergency alert flags and data-safe protocols for psychological trauma. Interventions must always be supportive, not prescriptive, acting as a companion rather than a replacement for clinical professionals.
